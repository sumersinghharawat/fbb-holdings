Driving positive change in our communities

Reyes Cares is a network of philanthropic programs across the Reyes Family of Businesses built to drive positive change in the communities where we do business.

Our Commitment

Our Reyes Cares banner embodies our commitment to make a positive impact in the communities where we live and work

As part of our commitment, we believe in the power and necessity of working together to lift up those around us and leave a better world for future generations.

Reyes Cares encompasses four focus areas where we feel we can have the most meaningful impact:

Community

Whether it be through volunteerism, product donations, philanthropic support, or partnerships, we strive to have a positive and lasting impact in our communities.

Recycling

We seek to support a circular economy and keep waste out of nature by finding innovative ways to encourage our employees to recycle.

Energy

Energy plays a critical role in our ability to be a global leader in the production and distribution of food and beverage products.

Fuel and Emissions

Through Reyes Fleet Management and their use of new technologies, we continue to make progress towards our goal of drastically reducing fuel use & emissions.
